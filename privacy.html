Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

The short version

Conversation Simulator runs entirely in your browser. The conversations you write, the names and photos you add, and the videos you export are processed locally on your device and are never uploaded to our servers. We don't have servers that store your content.

Data stored on your device

Your project (messages, participant names, photos and settings) is saved in your browser's localStorage so it's still there when you come back. You can erase it at any time by clearing your browser data for this site.

Analytics and advertising

We may use third-party advertising services such as Google AdSense to keep this tool free. These services may use cookies or similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visits to this site and/or other sites on the internet. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. For more information on how Google uses data, see policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

Cookies

The application itself does not set cookies. Third-party ad providers, when enabled, may set cookies as described above. Where required by law (e.g. EU/EEA visitors), a consent prompt is shown before any advertising cookies are used.

Children's privacy

This service is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Open an issue on the project's GitHub repository.
